Who can even be in the electorate

Before eligibility, registration, or documents, there is a simple gate. age. this step shows how much of the country is outside the voting universe from the start.

Who is legally eligible to vote

Voting age is not voting eligible. this step narrows to the pool used in turnout analysis, removing people who cannot legally vote.

Who is positioned to vote right now

Registration is the operational reality. it is the universe affected by registration rules, and it is the base most people assume when they talk about “voters.”

Who already has an easy proof-of-citizenship document

The SAVE Act discussion often collapses into documents. this step shows how many people have one commonly accepted proof in hand, without extra cost, time, or bureaucracy.

Who may face extra friction even if they are citizens

This is about mismatch. not intent. if proof depends on a birth certificate, a name change can create a paperwork trap where citizenship is real but hard to prove quickly.
