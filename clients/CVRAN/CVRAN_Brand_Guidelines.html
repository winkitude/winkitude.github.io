Brand overview

Central Vermont Refugee Action Network

CVRAN connects people seeking safety with the resources and community they need to rebuild their lives in Central Vermont. Our work is practical, relational, and rooted in the belief that everyone deserves a place to belong.

This document is the source of truth for anyone creating materials on behalf of CVRAN — staff, volunteers, and vendors alike.

Identity

Logo system

The CVRAN logo exists in two forms: a stacked mark (icon + "CVRAN") and a horizontal lockup (icon + full name). Use the full lockup wherever space permits. Maintain clear space equal to the height of the "C" on all sides.

Clear space & minimum size

Minimum clear space
Clear zone = cap-height of the "C" on all four sides. Minimum size: 180px wide (full lockup), 80px wide (stacked mark).

Typography

Primary typeface: Avenir Next. Avenir Next is a system font on Apple devices (macOS, iOS). For Windows and web, declare the full fallback stack. Do not substitute a different typeface for digital materials where the stack is available.

Usage

Imagery & icons

Photography should feel warm, documentary, and human — never generic stock. Center real people, real places, and real moments in Vermont.

Photography principles

PrincipleGuidance
People-firstFeature real community members and volunteers where consent is given. Candid and in-context outperforms posed. Never reduce individuals to their refugee status.
Place-groundedVermont landscapes, neighborhoods, and community spaces anchor the sense of welcome. Seasonal imagery is always relevant.
Warm, natural lightPrefer natural daylight or warm artificial light. Avoid cold blue casts, harsh studio setups, or heavy filters. Skin tones should be accurate and warm.
Color harmonyImages with greens, warm neutrals, and natural earth tones complement the brand palette best.
What to avoidGeneric stock photography, imagery that sensationalizes displacement, heavily filtered photos, low-res files, AI-generated human imagery.

Text on images

Always use a dark scrim (60–70% opacity) when placing text over photography to ensure legibility.

Icons

Use line-style icons in Green or Black. Stroke weight: 1.5–2px. Avoid filled bubble icons, gradient icons, and drop shadows. Recommended libraries: Lucide, Phosphor (regular weight).
