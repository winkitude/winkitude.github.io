Eurasia Group

For more than ten years I’ve shaped the visual voice of Eurasia Group by leading creative direction, building the brand, and managing a talented team of designers and freelancers. I’ve worked across everything from flagship products like Top Risks and the Atlas of Impunity to the quick-turn charts and reports that make our analysis clear and accessible.

I focus on setting a clear visual standard and making sure our design systems keep pace with the business. That means bringing consistency to the details, pushing for clarity in how ideas are communicated, and making sure design is part of the bigger strategy.
