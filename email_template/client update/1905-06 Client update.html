This Week's Research

While the country was focused largely on the Mueller reports and Joe Biden’s candidacy, here’s what our political risk analysts were focused on this week:

1) Geo-tech Report: China: Financing Innovation, The Shanghai Sci-Tech Innovation Board and china's New industrial Policy
China’s next generation of technology unicorns will come to market this summer via a long-awaited Nasdaq-style stock exchange: the Shanghai Sci-Tech Innovation Board (SSTIB). The board will allow some as-yet unprofitable firms to list through new registration and share price valuation procedures.

2) Japan/US Note: Breakthrough on “early harvest” trade deal unlikely at this week’s summit
Prime Minister Shinzo Abe’s discussions with President Donald Trump this week will do little to calm fears in Tokyo that the US is making tough demands of Japan while offering little in return
