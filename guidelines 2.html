Guidelines & Systems



I build guidelines that help teams move fast without losing quality—clear rules, reusable templates, and examples that scale across brands, video, and data visualization. Below are a few lanes that show how I use systems to raise the floor and keep the work consistent.

Lanes: Eurasia Group GZERO GZERO Video Charts

Eurasia Group – Brand system


I led the build-out of a practical brand system that could flex from flagship reports to daily client materials. My focus was on clarity and adoption: logo rules, color and type scales, layout grids, and downloadable assets that let analysts and designers produce consistent work quickly.

Outcomes: faster turnarounds, fewer revisions, and a shared visual language across reports and campaigns.

GZERO – Brand development


I evolved the GZERO identity for a fast editorial environment—daily news, long‑form, events, and partnerships. The guidelines balance strong recognition with room to move, so producers and designers can ship quickly without breaking the brand.

Charts & data visualization standards


I standardized chart typography, color ramps, gridlines, and source/notes hierarchy—and built Excel/PPT templates so non‑designers could produce work that stays readable and on brand. These rules show up everywhere from daily briefs to flagship reports.
