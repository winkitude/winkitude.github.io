The global roots of the
2026 World Baseball Classic

data pulled March 16, 2026

There has been some discussion about diaspora players in the World Baseball Classic. Eligibility rules allow players to represent a country through ancestry, citizenship, or family ties, so national teams are often built from players born in multiple places.

That dynamic is not unique to the tournament. Major League Baseball itself is international, though still heavily American. About 74% of players on 2024 Opening Day rosters were born in the United States.

World Baseball Classic rosters look different. Only about a third of players in the 2026 field were born in the United States, with the rest coming from a wide range of countries and territories.
