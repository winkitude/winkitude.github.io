Who he was

A Frenchton with a big personality.

Pepper was half French Bulldog, half Boston Terrier, and entirely one of a kind. He had opinions, routines, and a very specific set of priorities — none of which included long walks, but all of which included you. He brought silliness into every room and left a permanent mark on everyone he met.

Favourite Memories

Serious business

The Bubble Sessions

Pepper treated bubbles with the focus of a professional. The moment the bottle came out, he was locked in — zeroed, deadly serious, in his element. Every bubble was a threat that needed handling.

Core personality

Ball, Always Ball

He didn't just like ball. He was committed to it. No distractions, no half-hearted chasing. When the ball came out, nothing else existed.

Strategic genius

The Table Negotiator

He had a talent for appearing at dinner with maximum strategic visibility, looking at you in a way that made you question all of your choices. He rarely got table food. He never stopped trying.

Walk philosophy

Short Walks, Big Personality

The perfect Pepper walk was just long enough to sniff everything important and short enough to get back to the couch. He had it all figured out.

"A silly boy. A little potato. And we loved every bit of him." Pepper  ·  Forever a good boy
