Brand overview

Eurasia Group is the leading global research and advisory firm. We live in an era of volatility and uncertainty, one that generates both risks and opportunities for businesses and investors. The Eurasia Group logo reflects this view by using the shape of our initials to symbolize a fragmented globe. We are committed to helping clients understand and navigate this changing geopolitical landscape and make better informed decisions in an uncertain world.

The idea of "politics first" is central to our work. Politics is the lens through which we view the world and the starting point for our interactions with clients. Much has changed as Eurasia Group has expanded, but the politics first approach—and the commitment to analysis that is free of political bias and the influence of private interests—has not.

Logo system

Use the primary logo whenever possible. Maintain clear space equal to half the logomark square on all sides; if the mark is 50px × 50px, the clearance is 25px. Do not modify, distort, or recolor beyond approved variations.

Do
Primary logo on white

Keep clear space and minimum size intact. Use approved color variations only.

Don't
Stretched, recolored, or crowded

Avoid stretching, drop shadows, outlines, or placing on low‑contrast backgrounds.

Typography

Primary font: Acumin Pro. Alternatives: Industry, Source Serif for editorial accents, Arial/Calibri for generic fallback. Use consistent weight hierarchies and maintain generous line‑height.

RoleFontWeightsNotes
HeadlinesAcumin ProBold / BlackTight letter‑spacing at large sizes.
BodyAcumin ProRegular / Medium16–18px web, 1.5 line‑height.
Editorial pullSource SerifRegular / BoldUse sparingly.

Iconography

Use a consistent stroke weight and corner radii. Align to a 24px or 32px grid. Avoid overly detailed metaphors.

Do
Grid‑aligned, consistent stroke
Don't
Random sizes, mixed styles

Photography

Favor imagery that conveys clarity amid complexity, with authentic global context. Maintain sufficient contrast for overlaid type.

Accessibility

Text on color must meet WCAG AA. Prefer navy or blue‑1 on white for body text. Reserve cyan/gold for accents and large display only. Ensure focus states, alt text, and semantic HTML.

PairingUseStatus
Navy on whiteBody textAA+
Cyan on whiteButtons, large headlinesCheck size
Gold on whiteAccents onlyNot for body
