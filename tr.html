Top Risks



Top Risks is Eurasia Group’s biggest project of the year. Tens of thousands read the report. Millions more see the charts and graphics on TV and online.

Every January, we launch the report as the year kicks off. Research and writing start in the fall, but design only gets the green light about four weeks before publication. That window keeps shrinking because the world doesn’t slow down—major global events are happening more often, constantly shifting the main themes and upending our understanding right up to the finish line.

My role is part creative lead, part project manager, part fixer. I oversee and coordinate a thousand moving pieces—charts, layouts, photo collages, marketing campaigns, event graphics, and all the little details that make the whole package work. I keep the brand and vision consistent, consult on and directly design key visuals, and jump in wherever I’m needed. That means handling triage, solving last-minute problems, building assets, and making sure every piece—big or small—hits the mark and makes sense in the bigger story.

The sprint is fast, the stakes are high, and the project only works if every piece fits. I make sure it does.
