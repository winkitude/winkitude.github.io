Top Risks



Top Risks is Eurasia Group’s biggest project of the year. Tens of thousands read the report. Millions more see the charts and graphics on TV and online.

Every January, we release the report right as the year kicks off. The research and writing start in the fall, but I don’t get the design files until about four weeks before publication. That window keeps shrinking because the world doesn’t slow down. New, large-scale global events are happening more often, constantly shifting the main themes and upending our understanding—right up until the last minute.

My role: I’m responsible for everything visual—charts, data viz, layouts, photo collages, marketing materials, email graphics, and all the live event/broadcast assets. I set the look, handle the details, and get everything out the door, fast.

All the design work happens in about a month—usually less. That means clear priorities, fast turnarounds, and staying flexible as the world changes under your feet.
