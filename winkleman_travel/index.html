— For my father —

Before the boarding pass, before the e-ticket, there was the validation plate — a small embossed card pressed onto carbon tickets at the agency counter. My father kept a drawer full of them. Each one carried an airline's identity in miniature: its mark, its numbers, its moment in the century of commercial flight. These are the ones he saved.
