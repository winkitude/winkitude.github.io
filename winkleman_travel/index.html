— A Note from the Archivist —

Before the boarding pass, before the e-ticket, there was the validation plate — a small embossed card pressed onto carbon tickets at the agency counter. Each plate carried an airline's identity in miniature: its mark, its numbers, its moment in the century of commercial flight. What follows is the Winkleman Travel collection, preserved and presented in full.
